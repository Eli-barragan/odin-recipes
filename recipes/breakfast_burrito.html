Breakfast Burrito

Filled with spicy sausage, smoky scrambled eggs, shredded cheese, and a fresh avocado, these breakfast burritos are easy to make and a great way to start your day

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • Spinkle of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 of a spicy sausage link
  • 1/2 cup of chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of spinach
  • 1/4 of a large avocado
  • 1/4 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1 large tortilla

Steps

  1. Prep
    • In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs with the salt, pepper, and the red pepper flakes
    • Wash spinach, chop mushrooms and slice avocado and them all aside
  2. Cook the filling
    • Heat a large nonstick pan over medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook, stirring frequently until lightly browned, 2 to 3 minutes.
    • Add mushrooms to pan with sausage, stirring frequently until lightly browned, 1 to 2 min
    • Add spinach to mixture stirring frequently for another 30 seconds
    • Add eggs to mixture, stirring frequently until desired consistancy
  3. Assemble the burritos
    • Add cheese to center of tortilla
    • Add filling mixture to tortilla
    • Add avocado slices
    • Fold in the sides of the tortilla over the filling and roll, tucking in the edges as you go
  4. Toast the burrito
    • Lightly coat another pan with oil and set over medium heat
    • When the pan is hot, add the burritos, seam side down
    • Cook, covered, until the bottom of the burritos are golden brown, about 3 minutes
    • Flip the burritos over and continue cooking, covered, until golden, a few minutes more. Serve warm
  5. Enjoy!
